About Game

It’s a game of mighty heroes, of powerful foes, a game of strength, and honor, and of unlimited adventure. Now, you can check out a chronicle of the major events that happened. This exiting game consists of two characters - hero and troll. Your main goal is to destroy the enemy. You have several ways how to win the game: to solve mathematic tasks, to compare letters in words and others. When your answers is not correct, anemy will destroy you piece by piece. Game starting from pushing button "Attack", you make a decision about way of fight - math or logic, right answers will kill troll and hero will win! Don't forget to use your brain :)
